A Note from the President

For over 20 years, Promotion Plus Sign Co., Inc. has been committed to satisfying and delivering quality signage products and services to its customers.  As a full service sign company, we have been serving both independent and major commercial brands with a concentration in the Oil Industry across the United States since 1993.

Over the years, finding a formula that works in today’s rapidly changing economy has been challenging. The key to our success has been by focusing on innovative ways to better serve our clients, and providing the best possible products and services at competitive rates. With special thanks to our loyal customers, and through the sheer determination of our devoted management team, we are proud to announce that we are the oil industry’s leading #1 “Imaging Experts.”

The stepping stones to success are forged by the committed and dedicated in which our company continuously strives for and pursues. That being said, our core management team is here to serve you every step of the way, by providing a personable experience that is tailored to your signage needs.

We look forward to working with you soon.

All the best,

Tony Mastrangelo,
President
